# Related Works in UAV-assisted IoT Detection

## 1. UAV-assisted Wireless Sensor Networks (WSN)
UAVs are increasingly used to enhance coverage, connectivity, and data collection in WSNs. Research focuses on mobility models, energy efficiency, and dynamic topology management.

## 2. Fragmented Data Broadcast and Collection
Fragmented broadcast schemes improve reliability and robustness in IoT networks, especially in environments with intermittent connectivity. Studies explore optimal fragment sizes, scheduling, and recovery algorithms.

## 3. Alert Thresholds and Event Detection
Threshold-based detection is a common approach for anomaly or event recognition in distributed sensor networks. Related works analyze adaptive thresholding, false alarm reduction, and multi-node consensus.
